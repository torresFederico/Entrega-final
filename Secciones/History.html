THE HISTORY OF THE RACING BULLS

Red Bull Racing came into existence late in 2004 under the guidance of team principal Christian Horner, himself new to F1, with ambitions to challenge for grand prix victories and world championship titles. Nine championships later, the legend is still going on strong, and the bulls are still fighting to get it all!
